Visual Hierarchy
Healthgrades
https://www.healthgrades.com

We can see a clear example of visual hierarchy here as the company focuses on its main service offering and source of income, the doctor search, followed quickly by the doctor reviews. By placing the doctor search at the top of the page and targeting it to get the most engagement they optimize their revenue stream. The second item on the page represents a secondary entry point to the same revenue stream.

Repetition
Spire Digital
https://www.gotrax.com

As a sales site the key here is repetition of a stylized representation of each product. Each product is presented with a consistent look and feel, detail presentation and layout.

White Space and Clean Design
Gogo Business Aviation
https://business.gogoair.com

Even though much of the 'whitespace here is in Red, this site shows an impressive consciousness of a balance between content and clean open spaces between content. The blocks of text are lear and lean, staying close in proximity but leaving plenty of room for readability and to feel crisp and not cluttered.
