Krysta Hunt - WDD-230

Target Audience:

The target audience for this site will be local and regional residents of southwestern Idaho. This will include both urban and residential residents of all income levels. Most will be literate and between the ages of 16-90. Most of them will likely be using mobile devices to access.

Scenario 1:

Martha Rae is hosting a bake sale fund raiser sponsered by a joint faith effort to raise money for those who are struggling in the area. She wants to post the event with date times, maps, a list of sponsors on the site calendar and local events page. Along with this the site should send an email update to all subscribed members about the community event a week, three days and the day before the event.

Scenario 2:

Henry Jones is trying to restore his parents placed after his mom passed and the house had a style of glass doorknobs that is unique to a builder in the area. He posts on the community trade boards for anyone selling or willing to trade for one of these door knobs to replace the one his sister broke off the bathroom when they were children.

Scenario 3:

Katie Lee would like to suprise her new husband with a picnic lunch near his office, they are new to the area so she wants to use the site to find a quiet romantic outdoor setting near his office. She is able to find a quiet park about three blocks from where he works and is also able to confirm that the weather on thirsday will be just right to spend the early afternoon outdoors.

Scenario 4:

Smboke Oyekunle is interested in signing his daughter up for dance lessons and his son for little league. He uses the site to see when and where the local teams practice as well as locate when a local dance studio offers classes, he is able to coordinate both with a local curling enthusiasts club that he and his wife can join together.

Scenario 5:

Rural farmers need a daily update on rain statistics for the past several years as well as a predictive model of upcoming rain projections for the current season. Reports of insect migrations, fluxuations in crops prices and avalability of local and migratory workers.

Scenario 6:

Hannibal has recently started volunteering at the local animal shelter and is posting pets available for adoption as well as helping provide lost pet posts when owners misplace their furry family members.
