Founder's Day Fesco

By Hercule Poirot

Come join the town of Preston as we celebrate the 112th year since the Town's founding. If you are new to Preston you may be interested to note that there are 3 founding fathers of the town, Samuel Eli Preston, James Preston Sammuels and Preston Elijah James. It has been One Hundred Twelve years, also known as five score and two days short of a fortyear. Our town has seen 28 leap years, two haley's commets and 112 partridges in 112 pear trees. Well be celebrating the birthday of Emelda Luellen James who turns 112 this Friday, she was the first little one officially born in the established town of Preston. There will be a fare in the town center, where we will be bobbong for 112 year old apples, capture the 112 flags, the three legged race and other old time classics. Make sure you don't miss out on the Faire Foods either, especially Mr's Graebner's funnel cake.
